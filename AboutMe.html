About Me

I am a current student at Champlain College, driven by an unwavering passion for Game Programming. My journey in programming has been a lifelong pursuit, but it is the ability to craft experiences that evoke emotions in people that truly ignites my enthusiasm. Games serve as my canvas for this artistic expression, and witnessing others derive joy from the worlds and experiences I create is an immensely gratifying experience that never loses its charm.

Beyond the realm of coding, I find my vitality in the great outdoors, immersing myself in a rich tapestry of activities that include hiking, skateboarding, and archery. Having been raised in the picturesque landscapes of Vermont, I have cultivated a profound connection with the natural world, viewing it as a sanctuary for personal rejuvenation.

For me, the art of collaboration and the dynamics of a well-coordinated team are among the true joys of this industry. Please feel free to reach out to me at any time.
