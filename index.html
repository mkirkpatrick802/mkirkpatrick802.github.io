About Me:

I am a current student at Champlain College, driven by a passion for Game Programming and a fascination with Applied Mathematics. My journey in programming has been a lifelong pursuit, but it is the ability to craft experiences that evoke genuine emotions in people that truly ignites my enthusiasm. Games serve as my canvas for this artistic expression, and witnessing others derive joy from the worlds and experiences I create is an immensely gratifying experience that never loses its charm.

Beyond the realm of coding, I find my vitality in the great outdoors, immersing myself in many activities that include hiking, skateboarding, and archery. Having been raised in a picturesque town in rural Vermont, I have cultivated a profound connection with the natural world, viewing it as a sanctuary for personal rejuvenation.

In the games industry, teamwork is not just a preference but a cornerstone of success, and it holds great significance for me. Collaborating with diverse talents, sharing creative visions, and working cohesively to bring game experiences to life is where my passion thrives. I'm always eager to connect with fellow game devs who share this appreciation for the power of teamwork. Let's unite and create something extraordinary together.
